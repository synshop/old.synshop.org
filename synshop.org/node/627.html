State if the Shop - September 2015 - We're Moving! Need Volunteers

By Krux

Big news.  For those who attended the last board meeting, you already know that we have been looking at a new location and have been in lease negotiations.

As of today, we have a signed lease, and should be getting the keys to the space this Tuesday.

That's right we finally are moving!  SYN Shop has a new location.  The address is 1075 American Pacific, Suite C, Henderson, NV 89074

https://goo.gl/maps/swQbG

Our plan is to move the bulk of everything this Saturday the 19th.  We'll need lots of help, so now is the time to volunteer, and assist with the great move to SYN Shop 2.0.

Prior to Saturday.  More packing.  We have a lot to do, so time spent packing before the move will be time we don't have to spend doing it on Saturday.  This is also a good option for members who can't make it Saturday, but can be at the shop earlier in the week.

Saturday morning, we are going to be meeting up at the down town location (117 N. 4th St.) at 10am.  We'll be packing all the things that need to be packed, and loading everything into trucks and trailers, and then unpacking it at the new location.

Here is the marked up floor plan of where we expect to put things initially.  We do know this will change, but we first need to be in the space to learn works and what doesn't, as well as some compromises until we can raise money to have additional office space built out (where the carpeted area that is open to one end of the space warehouse is located)

https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B3zz3OgQxXgySVd0cmhicnUwOU0/view?usp=sharing

From that point forward, any events or open nights that were being hosted down town will be at the new location.

I look forward to the move and the awesome things we will accomplish in our new location, and I hope you are just as excited.
