Steve Jobs Seance 2015

By Automated Importer

Saturday Oct 31, 2015 6:00 pm

It's that time of year when the realm of the spirits beyond eclipses with that of the living... This also means that we're all in danger of the restless ghost of Steve Jobs haunting our Apple products once more.

TWO YEARS AGO a small group of brave SYN Shop members gathered together and assisted in a seance to banish the irritable Steve from a defective laptop. There were cookies and beer, and a lot of fun was had by all... but most importantly, their efforts were effective. We haven't seen much of Steve since then... and our laptops and peripherals have been working at peak performance.

Let's make sure that during this month, the door is not left open for the ghost of Steve to return and make residence in our new home here in Henderson.

If you're like me... and don't have anything exciting going on this Halloween, I invite you to bring one piece of broken or obsolete technology as an offering and wear the appropriate garb of a black turtleneck to SYN Shop for a night of cookies, beer, and Apple parodies.

I'll light some candles, we'll have some laughs, and by all means bring candy to much on if you like. Depending on how things go, maybe we'll even watch a scary movie in the dark... I'm open to suggestions.

The fun will begin at 6:00pm on the Saturday of Halloween and go until we're satiated.

PS, we all got our turtlenecks from thrift stores! Easy costume! ;)

I hope to see you there!

-Sarah
