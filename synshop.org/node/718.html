Beer Cooler

By Tim Gibson

I've been working on a number of brewing projects and wanted to share a recent 3D print I've created. This is a support bracket for the serving lines inside the chest freezer adapted to work as a keg chiller. Since 8 feet of serving line is recommended to allow low foaming pours I needed to mount the lines from the taps in the front around the inside of the cooler to the back. This required 6 of these mounting brackets. They will accommodate up to 15mm lines and the mounting holes are 6mm to work with a #6 machine screw. As side note, the Creator Dual is working nicely with the Red filament. I found a temp of 210c, media diameter of 1.73mm, and a bed heated to 50c produces good results
