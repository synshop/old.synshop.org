Soldering Workshop: Building a Digital Clock

By Automated Importer

Saturday May 28, 2016 1:00 pm

This class is perfect for beginners, and is a fun project for all skill levels!
Students will learn bedrock skills that enable electronic tinkering and ideas for furthering their self-education.   Each student will leave with a fully functioning clock soldered from a kit of components.
