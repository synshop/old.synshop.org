Continuing Regular Shop Hours!

By James C

Just wanted to post a quick heads' up, we now have regular shop hours for all members:

  • Mondays 6pm - 10pm
  • Thursdays 6pm - 10pm
  • Saturdays 3pm - 10pm

Members who are vetted obviously will continue to have access at any time :) 

The hours have actually been somewhat regular for the past few weeks, but since we weren't sure how those days and hours would work out, we just treated them as Meetup events until we were sure they'd stick.

So in order to unclutter our Meetup feed, I'll be removing those extraneous events; We should be using Meetup events as intended going forward instead of just to post our "regular business hours".

We just wanted to let everyone know why those events are going away!

So come on down, and Let's Make Stuff Awesome!
