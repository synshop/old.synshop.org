Members Only Class - MIG Welding Hands-on w/ Richard Furniss

By Automated Importer

Saturday Jun 27, 2015 9:00 am

Join Richard Furniss in this hands on class using MIG Welding equipment where you will learn welding steel with a MIG type welder to create your own metal art sculpture.

STUDENTS WILL NEED TO ENSURE THEY COME PROPERLY ATTIRED FOR SAFETY:
This means cotton clothing, full cover of arms and legs, sun glasses and no open-toed shoes. Failure to do so could mean you get turned away from the class or injured.
