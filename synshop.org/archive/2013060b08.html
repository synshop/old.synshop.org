Tinkercad 101

By Automated Importer

Friday Jun 28, 2013 7:00 pm

Tinkercad is back and we're teaching a class on it!

For those of you who do not know, Tinkercad is this awesome web based 3D CAD program. It was slated to go offline later this year. However, Autodesk just bought it and it will remain online. Yeah!

It is by far the easiest to use 3D CAD program that we have encountered. Additionally, you can export the file to a format that can be used on the Shop's 3D printers.

Shapeways Meetup - Elasto Plastic Debut!

By Automated Importer

Wednesday Jul 17, 2013 7:00 pm

After the success of the last two Shapeways meetups, a third will be held to give attendees a sneak peek of an exciting new material you'll be able to 3D print in very soon - Elasto Plastic! This soft, flexible, squishy material has huge potential for use in creative tech and art projects. Watch bands, seals, and soft electronics comes to mind when considering this material.

Come and play with Elasto Plastic (and all of the other cool materials Shapeways offers) and geek out over 3D printing with likeminded curious people.

http://www.meetup.com/synshop/events/124719572/
