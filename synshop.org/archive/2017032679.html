Class: Crypto-Party

By Automated Importer

15 Mar 2017

The Fauzy Hour: Placentophagy and You (brought to you by Jing)

By Automated Importer

Friday Mar 17, 2017 5:30 pm

Placenta Pills for Postpartum Health? Description: While mothers in almost every mammalian species routinely ingest the placenta after giving birth, this behavior seems to have emerged only recently in humans. In this talk, I will discuss which human populations consume the placenta postpartum, how and why women are doing it, and the effects on health and postpartum recovery.

The Fauzy Hour is an off the cuff talk / discussion / demo led by a person with knowledge of the subject matter.

https://www.meetup.com/synshop/events/238353447/
