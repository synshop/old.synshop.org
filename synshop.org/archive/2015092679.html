18 Sep 2015

SYN Shop Move Day

By Automated Importer

Saturday Sep 19, 2015 10:00 am

We're moving SYN Shop to our new location at 1075 American Pacific Drive, Suite C, Henderson NV 89074.

Our plan is to move the bulk of everything this Saturday the 19th. We'll need lots of help, so now is the time to volunteer, and assist with the great move to SYN Shop 2.0.

On Saturday our rally point is going to be at the down town location (117 N. 4th St.) Richard is bringing the truck to the shop at 8am, so we'll have someone there a little early with donuts to get started.

Lockpicking Night - Open Night

By Automated Importer

Monday Sep 14, 2015 6:00 pm
