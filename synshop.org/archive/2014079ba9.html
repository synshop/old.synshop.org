Fix-it Night

By Automated Importer

Thursday Apr 23, 2015 6:00 pm


Synshop is having a  "Fix It" night, one night a month.   Bring something from home you are having trouble with, and tap into the community to fix it. If no one brings anything in we will also perform preventative maintenance and repair on the shop equipment. 


Examples of repairable items include: a phone, computer, drill, printer, bike, furniture, lawn mower, cars, , models, toys, clothing etc..

Dial Indicator Magnetic Base

By billt

I had some time this weekend to make a small magnetic base for my G0602 Grizzly lathe.  The lathe doesn't have a DRO (Digital Read Out) so I've been thinking about using my 1" travel dial indicator to make the cuts more precise.  I watch a lot of the machine shop channels on YouTube and a few of the guys just use a simple mag base stuck to the ways.  It didn't seem that difficult to make one and I had some 7/16" diameter neodymium magnets lying around.

10 Jul 2014

Shopbot 100

By Automated Importer

Tuesday Jul 22, 2014 6:45 pm

This class will be led by our very own Chris Oremus.


This introductory class will cover the following:

     Shopbot operation: job setup, fixtures, tools, safety, cleanup

     Job set up: coordinate reference, CNC principles, hot key shortcuts

     Basic woodworking theory: which bits and why

     Example runs for each student: each student will learn to load material, load a job, and will take home a design cut from 1/2in MDF
