Hack Your Hackerspace

By Automated Importer

Saturday Apr 20, 2019 3:00 pm

We're going to be having a shop cleanup on Saturday April 20th at 3pm. Our goal is to clean and de-clutter the shop, primarily in preparation for our Science is Everywhere Day event August 28th at 11am.

- Vacuum all carpets
- Sweep and mop danger room floor
- Organize shelves and storage
- Put all tools away
- Dust and clean all machines in the danger room
- Get rid of trash and other junk
- Take home member projects or materials which are not actively being worked on

11 Apr 2019

The Fauzy Hour: (brought to you by Jing)Life is Beautiful and Veterans Suicide.

By Automated Importer

Friday Apr 12, 2019 6:00 pm

Please join us as our fellow Synshopper Lito recounts a personal story in the working life of a network engineer for the US special operations command.

A story that balances high-speed operations enabled by military technological capacity, we dive deep into the hearts and minds of human possibility, and the struggle to understand ourselves and people just like us.

No recording allowed.

The Fauzy Hour is an off the cuff talk / discussion / demo led by a person with knowledge of the subject matter.
