The Fauzy Hour: Radio Adventures (brought to you by Jing)

By Automated Importer

Friday Mar 24, 2017 5:30 pm

Few technologies have impacted society to the degree that radio has. Radio plays a vital part in almost every human being's life in one way or another. Most people are oblivious to how it works, what all is being sent out over the airwaves, and the privacy and security implications of these methods of communication. This talk aims to be a brief introduction to radio from a hacker's point of view.

The Fauzy Hour is an off the cuff talk / discussion / demo led by a person with knowledge of the subject matter.
