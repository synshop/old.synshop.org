Intermediate 3D Design With Autodesk Fusion 360

By Automated Importer

Thursday May 7, 2015 7:00 pm

Learn to use Autodesk's Fusion 360 software to turn
your idea into a 3D model that's ready for the laser cutter, Shopbot, or 3D printer. 

Please Note: Students must already be comfortable with basic 3D modeling using software like TinkerCAD, SketchUp, or Blender as this class focuses on more advanced techniques.

Prerequisites:

• Laptop 

• Mouse
