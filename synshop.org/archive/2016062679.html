ShopBot Like A Boss (Members Only)

By Automated Importer

Saturday Jun 18, 2016 4:30 pm

This is a free class for paying SYN Shop members only.  Due to space and time constraints we can only accommodate up to four people at a time around the ShopBot.

Looking to get started in the wonderful worlds of CNC routing?  Looking to learn more about the ShopBot so you can understand more about it, other than it being a tool that somehow you put plywood into and furniture comes out.  This is an introduction to running the ShopBot, the CNC sheet router, we have at SYN Shop.

17 Jun 2016

LV STEAM Explorers: Leather Working 101

By Automated Importer

Saturday Jun 18, 2016 10:30 am

Las Vegas STEAM Explorers presents Leather Working 101! 

This class will show your child the techniques of basic leather working by making a leather cuff or bracelet. The teacher, Caitlin Wolf, will be going over how to use decorative stamps and coloring the leather with dyes. This is a hands-on workshop and parent participation is encouraged. The cost of the class includes materials and your child will be able to take home their creation!

http://www.meetup.com/synshop/events/231372692/

DEFCON Badge Prep Night

By Automated Importer

Thursday Jun 16, 2016 7:00 pm

*** Open to folks at ALL Skill levels***


At DEFCON, and other conferences, having a special blingy/blinky badge that only you and only a few other people have will auto-magicly increase your street cred. 

This meetup is to plan coordinate and eventually fabricate the creation of a SYNSHOP specific hardware conference badge. Come with your hardware know-how, art knowledge, or a willingness to learn so we can share good ideas, and be ready to help make something cool.
