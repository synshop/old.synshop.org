Shapeways & 3D Printing Meetup

By Automated Importer

Sunday Jun 2, 2013 3:00 pm

Savannah Peterson is taking Shapeways on tour and her first stop is Downtown Las Vegas! Join us at SYN Shop this Sunday from 3-5 PM for a talk about Shapeways, 3D printing technology, and what you can do to get started. Following the talk, we will hold a Q&A session and a meet and greet. Bring your Shapeways or 3D printed projects to show off or bring your sketchbook and consult with others about what you'd like to create. Stick around for drinks at Denny's afterward if you like!

18 May 2013

Tinkercad 101: It's Back!

By Automated Importer

Friday May 24, 2013 7:00 pm

Tinkercad is back and we're teaching a class on it!

For those of you who do not know, Tinkercad is this awesome web based 3D CAD program. It was slated to go offline later this year. However, Autodesk just bought it and it will remain online. Yeah!

It is by far the easiest to use 3D CAD program that we have encountered.  Additionally, you can export the file to a format that can be used on the Shop's 3D printers.
