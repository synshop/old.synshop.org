28 Feb 2016

3D Printing 101

By Automated Importer

Monday Feb 29, 2016 6:00 pm

3d printing Guru Andrew Morrow is teaching a class on 3D printing basics!

The class covers:

-  Anatomy of a 3D printer
-  Safety when operating the machine
-  Storage and treatment of consumables
-  Basic maintenance of the machine
-  Troubleshooting a variety of potential failures
-  Available software to send prints to the machine
-  Tips for leveling the build plate

http://www.meetup.com/synshop/events/228753310/

Las Vegas Mini Maker Faire

By Automated Importer

Sunday Feb 28, 2016 12:00 pm

A mini version of the Greatest Show (and Tell) on Earth—a family-friendly showcase of invention, creativity and resourcefulness, and a celebration of the Maker Movement. It’s a place where people show what they are making, and share what they are learning.

This year, we're holding it at the Henderson Convention Center on Sunday, February 28th from Noon to 6pm. Early Bird Tickets are on sale now at www.makerfairevegas.com.

Fix-it Night

By Automated Importer

Thursday Feb 25, 2016 6:00 pm


Synshop is having a  "Fix It" night, one night a month.   Bring something from home you are having trouble with, and tap into the community to fix it. If no one brings anything in we will also perform preventative maintenance and repair on the shop equipment. 


Examples of repairable items include: a phone, computer, drill, printer, bike, furniture, lawn mower, cars, , models, toys, clothing etc..
