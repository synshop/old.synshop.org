23 Apr 2013

Science Crawl

By Automated Importer

Thursday May 2, 2013 6:00 pm

As part of the Science and Technology Festival's week long events we are doing a science crawl.

6-7 David Knight, the producer of the film "Endeavor Comes Home" (it's going to be shown in theaters before the new Star Trek movie), chronicling the final journey of the Space Shuttle Endeavor from FL to its permanent new home at the California Science Center, will give a talk at the Downtown Construction Zone.

7-8 Then we head over to SynShop to make, hack and play. I am bringing DIY Light Saber materials!

http://www.meetup.com/synshop/events/115756902/

Light Saber Flash Mob at Science Festival

By Automated Importer

Saturday May 4, 2013 5:30 pm

This is the follow up to building Light Sabers in the Shop for Science Crawl!

The Las Vegas Science and Technology Festival falls this year on May 4th, Star Wars Day.

I wanted to do something really special for kids who are visiting us this year.

We have the Storm Trooper Battalion coming to the show, and what better way to honor this great collision of nerdy goodness than a Light Saber Flash Mob at a Science Festival on Star Wars Day.

http://www.meetup.com/synshop/events/115756412/
