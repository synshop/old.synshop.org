Intro to Power Supplies

By Automated Importer

Saturday Sep 14, 2013 12:00 pm

 Course Title: Basic Power Supplies


 Skills to be learned:

Differences between linear and switching power supplies

Main parts of a power supply

Building a basic AC to DC linear power supply

Ohm's Law Paid Class

By Automated Importer

Wednesday Sep 11, 2013 7:00 pm

This class is being offered by Mack who is going to walk us through arguably one of the most important aspects of electronics--Ohm's Law.  By the end you will feel at peace with the resistance.  This is a paid class that raises funds for SYN Shop!  If you have a Multimeter please please please bring it.  


Skills to be learned:

What is Ohm's Law?


What is Volts and how to measure it.


What is Current how to measure it.


What is resistance and how to measure it.

http://www.meetup.com/synshop/events/134558442/

Meet the Teachers - August

By Automated Importer
