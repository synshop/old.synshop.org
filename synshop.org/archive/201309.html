23 Sep 2013

Lighting Basics Presentation

By Automated Importer

Thursday Oct 10, 2013 6:00 pm

Lighting is changing rapidly, with new light sources emerging in the market and traditional sources changing to become more efficient. Join us to learn about lighting technology, from improvements to the incandescent lamps we have known to the newest LED lamps. Learn which lamps are being phased out and what replacements will be available, or just come to learn more about the future of lighting.

This class is held in conjunction with the Illuminating Engineering Society. There is no charge for this presentation.

18 Sep 2013

555 Timer Intro (Rescheduled from 9-18-13)

By Automated Importer

Wednesday Sep 25, 2013 6:30 pm

Originally designed by Hans Camenzind and first manufactured by Signetics in 1971, the 555 timer is an integrated circuit that predates the microprocessor, but is still in widespread use today. Since 2003, worldwide sales and manufacturing of this 8-pin chip now exceed one billion units every year, for use in a wide variety of timing circuits, such as pulse generators, oscillators, flip-flops, and more.
