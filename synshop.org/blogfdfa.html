RePurpose America

By billt

We met with Zachary Delbex of RePurpose America on Saturday and took a tour of his warehouse.  His non-profit takes materials left over from trade shows in Las Vegas and "repurposes" them into other things.  His warehouse is like candyland.  Everywhere you look, there's something cool to play with.  We're looking forward to working with Zach on some projects.
