Soldering Workshop: SparkFun Gram Piano Kit

By Automated Importer

Saturday Nov 12, 2016 2:00 pm

For our next Soldering Workshop, we'll be assembling the Gram Piano kit from Sparkfun Electronics.

This kit provides you with the ability to play an octave worth of notes using the capacitive touch keys

The Gram Piano is a simple through-hole kit with a fair part count, making it ideal for beginning solderers looking for a little more challenge. Building and playing with this kit will teach you various skills related to soldering, electronics, and programming.

09 Nov 2016

The Fauzy Hour: Rapid Protyping with Fusion 360 (brought to you by Jing)

By Automated Importer

Friday Nov 11, 2016 5:30 pm

Have you ever wanted to make a custom widget for one of your projects, but got put off by the learning curve and hassle of commercial CAD software? In this talk, we’ll cover basic principles of parametric CAD and show how one can design a simple but functional 3D-printable part in only a few minutes with Fusion 360. We'll demonstrate how to quickly bring in CAD models from various sources to integrate existing hardware into your design, and compare various prototyping methods so you can choose the best process for your requirements.

http://www.meetup.com/synshop/events/235052656/

3D Printing 101

By Automated Importer

Monday Nov 7, 2016 6:00 pm

3d printing Guru Andrew Morrow is teaching a class on 3D printing basics!

The class covers:

-  Anatomy of a 3D printer
-  Safety when operating the machine
-  Storage and treatment of consumables
-  Basic maintenance of the machine
-  Troubleshooting a variety of potential failures
-  Available software to send prints to the machine
-  Tips for leveling the build plate
