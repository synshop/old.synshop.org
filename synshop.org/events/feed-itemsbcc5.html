SYN Shop Partluck

By Automated Importer

Friday Dec 12, 2014 7:00 pm

Caleb and Vanessa are going to be hosting an awesome event called the SYN Shop Partluck.

What is a Partluck?  It's kind of like a potluck, but members are invited to bring old hardware and various pieces of upcycleable materials to share with other members.  These materials are then stripped down and built into various other projects as the night progresses.

Class: Basic Electronics 4 - Digital Logic & analog circuits in wearable tech

By Automated Importer

Saturday Jan 17, 2015 11:00 am

Class: Basic Electronics 4 - Mixing Digital Logic and Analog Circuits with a Wearable Technology Kit.

Another “wearable tech” DIY project! Build a wearable butterfly with two wings that oscillate by using a shape-memory-metal actuator. The instructional part of the class will also focus on digital logic and digital-to-analog conversion (DAC) circuitry:

Members Only Class - MIG Welding Hands-on w/ Richard Furniss

By Automated Importer

Saturday Jun 27, 2015 9:00 am

Join Richard Furniss in this hands on class using MIG Welding equipment where you will learn welding steel with a MIG type welder to create your own metal art sculpture.

STUDENTS WILL NEED TO ENSURE THEY COME PROPERLY ATTIRED FOR SAFETY:
This means cotton clothing, full cover of arms and legs, sun glasses and no open-toed shoes. Failure to do so could mean you get turned away from the class or injured.
