01 Oct 2016

3D Printing 101

By Automated Importer

Monday Oct 3, 2016 6:00 pm

3d printing Guru Andrew Morrow is teaching a class on 3D printing basics!

The class covers:

-  Anatomy of a 3D printer
-  Safety when operating the machine
-  Storage and treatment of consumables
-  Basic maintenance of the machine
-  Troubleshooting a variety of potential failures
-  Available software to send prints to the machine
-  Tips for leveling the build plate

http://www.meetup.com/synshop/events/233360094/

30 Sep 2016

Automation 101

By Automated Importer

Saturday Oct 1, 2016 1:00 pm

This class will cover the very basics on how you can turn your smart phone/tablet into an automation machine with the help of a few FREE apps. We will be covering how to harness the power of IFTTT for Android/iOS and Tasker for Android.

Automatically send a text message to loved ones when you arrive at a location safely.

Automatically update all of your social media with a single post.

Automatically grab news articles from your favorite sources.
