Soldering Workshop with the Timesquare DIY Watch Kit

By Automated Importer

Thursday May 8, 2014 6:30 pm

For our next soldering workshop, by popular demand, we are assembling the Adafruit Timesquare DIY Watch kit ( https://www.adafruit.com/products/1225 )

This watch comes with a ultra bright LED matrix and a black silicone watch band that fits all wrists from children to adult.  This is a DIY kit, and requires some basic soldering/assembly to put together. It is a beginner kit, so this is a fine project to use in learning how to solder.

15 Apr 2014

Welding for Craft and Art - Intro to Oxy/Acetylene Welding

By Automated Importer

Saturday May 24, 2014 12:00 pm

Join Richard Furniss as he covers welding theory and safety using oxy/acetylene equipment.  This class is designed to provide enough information and examples to give the participant the confidence to try welding at home.  

This kind of welding can be done outside on a BBQ grill to create works of art.  Suggested material for home practice will be copper rods, flat copper sheets and thin metal tubing.

http://www.meetup.com/synshop/events/177354962/
  • Read more about Welding for Craft and Art - Intro to Oxy/Acetylene Welding
