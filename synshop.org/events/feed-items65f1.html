Sewing 101 - Part 1 (Intro to your sewing machine)

By Automated Importer

Sunday Jan 22, 2017 3:00 pm

Love your sewing machine and learn all of the sewing basics with Carlyne Michaelis!

This class is an 8 part series meeting on Sundays at 3pm starting on January 22nd and culminating with a tote bag project during the last class on March 12th. It is highly recommended that you sign up for all parts in the series and plan to attend all 8. This class covers all of the basics, including:

• how to use your sewing machine

• types of threads and needles

• how to hand sew various stitches

Class: Crypto-Party

By Automated Importer

Sunday Jan 15, 2017 1:00 pm

A Crypto Party is a gathering to learn and discuss computer Cryptographic tools, privacy, first amendment rights, and responsible legal methods of using these tools. This is an "Explain it like I'm 5 years old" (ELI5) level course.

The motivation is to empower regular people to protect their private data in an era of widespread surveillance. If you want to go further than just info, bring a functional laptop.
